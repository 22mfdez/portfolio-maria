My year in review 2023

By Maria Fernandez January 13, 2024

2023 Unveiled: Navigating Code, Love, and Seasons in Southern Spain

Reflecting on 2023, it's been a rollercoaster—learning new programming languages, tackling a mix of challenging and easy assignments, and enduring the extremes of winter cold and summer heat while savoring the beauty of Southern Spain. From my first scuba diving class to heartbreaks, it's been a journey filled with code, countless photos, and beach sunsets. Park visits with my dog Neo and summer nights on my friend's gardeen brought joy. Amidst a soundtrack of diverse music, the memories linger, shaping a year of growth and experiences. #2023Review #LifeJourney.
